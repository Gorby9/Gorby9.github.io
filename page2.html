Corticospinal Tract

PLS research is limited by the rarity of the disease, inhomegenity in patient symptoms, and lack of biomarkers.

PLS Natural History Study

There is currently a study underway to measure disease progression and biological markers in well-established and early PLS cases.It was initiated by Dr. Mitsumoto at Columbia University, but now involves multiple sites and funding sources.

Ozdinler Lab

Dr. Hande Ozdinler at Northwest University is a notable scientist in upper motor neuron research.
