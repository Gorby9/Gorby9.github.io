Don't fall

Don't fall

I've had PLS since 2011. It started when I was 47 years old, with speech problems and PBA. I currently use a rollator to get around the house and a wheelchair for outings. It takes me a long time to eat, but I haven't had to modify my diet. My speech is understandable about fifty percent of the time. Medications reduce some symptoms, but I haven't found anything to slow progression. Although PLS has spread to all of the muscles in my body, I am thankful that I have not lost complete function anywhere.
